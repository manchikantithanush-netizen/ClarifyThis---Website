Important: macOS Security Notice

Because ClarifyThis is an independent app and not downloaded from the Mac App Store, macOS may show a warning like:

“ClarifyThis can’t be opened because Apple cannot check it for malicious software.”

This is normal for new developer apps. To open ClarifyThis:
	1.	Try opening the app once (it will be blocked).
	2.	Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security.
	3.	Scroll down to the Security section.
	4.	You’ll see a message about ClarifyThis being blocked.
	5.	Click “Open Anyway.”
	6.	Confirm by clicking “Open.”

ClarifyThis does NOT contain malware and does NOT access your files without permission.
